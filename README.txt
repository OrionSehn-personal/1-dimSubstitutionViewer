-----------------------------------------------------------------------

	One Dimensional Substitution Viewer - Orion Sehn 2021

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
This project seeks to understand and provide a means to display and
explore 1 - dimensional substitution tilings.

The program includes a small set of substitution tilings, as well as
means to view information and different diagrams of them. You can also
define other 1 dim - substitutions.
